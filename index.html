About Me

I'm a Senior iOS Engineer With over 10 years of experience in software engineering. I am a passionate and proficient iOS Engineer at Glovo, one of the leading on-demand delivery platforms in Europe and Africa. I work as part of the Customer cluster, where I design, analyze, and implement new features and improve the customer experience while browsing the store, categories, and products. I do work on some other areas which are related to the order status and checkout process. I have a strong understanding of SOLID principles, design patterns, unit testing, git, code review, A/B testing, marketing, and analysis integrations, combine framework, RxSwift, and object-oriented programming (OOP) concepts. I have also acquired certifications from Coursera and PiTechnologies in software product management and iOS app development. Moreover, I have worked in multiple business fields, such as delivery systems, fintech/payment solutions, e-commerce, and others. I enjoy solving problems and designing solutions that are clean and scalable, and that align with the best practices and latest trends in the industry. My goal is to create products that deliver value and satisfaction to the users and the stakeholders.
